About Me

I'm Isiaka Olukayode Mosudi, a systems engineer, software developer, technical support specialist, and researcher with a strong interest in the intersection of software engineering, networking, and intelligent systems. My work connects theoretical research with practical engineering to build adaptive, data-driven, and connected systems.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a Master's in Communication Engineering. I am currently pursuing a Master's in IoT and Smart Systems at FH Technikum Wien, where my research focuses on cyber-physical systems, edge computing, and distributed intelligence in smart urban environments.

Professionally, my experience spans Network Infrastructure cloud infrastructure engineering, large-scale backend/API development, and sensor network integration. I have delivered solutions that integrate secure enterprise networking, Flask-based microservices, and automation pipelines designed for intelligent data flow, performance optimisation, and operational resilience.

I am driven by the mission of building technology that enhances quality of life: from IoT-enabled agricultural systems that support food security to digital platforms that empower multi-site enterprise environments, innovation, learning, and creative communities.
